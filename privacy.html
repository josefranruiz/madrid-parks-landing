Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Madrid Parks ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

🔒 Your Privacy Matters

Madrid Parks is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data and use it only to provide you with the best possible experience.

Information We Collect

Location Data

Madrid Parks requests access to your device's location to:

  • Show nearby parks and calculate distances
  • Provide relevant weather and pollen information for your area
  • Display parks in order of proximity to you

Important: Your location data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

App Usage Data

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to improve the app's performance and user experience. This data does not identify you personally.

No Personal Information

Madrid Parks does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • User accounts or profiles
  • Payment information
  • Social media data

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Provide real-time park status information
  • Display weather and environmental data for your location
  • Show pollen levels and allergy information
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Ensure app stability and performance

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties.

Data Sources

Madrid Parks integrates with public data sources:

  • Madrid City Council Open Data: Park status, alerts, and operational information
  • Madrid Regional Government: Weather data and pollen information
  • Public APIs: All data sources are publicly available and do not require authentication

These integrations provide real-time information but do not involve sharing your personal data.

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Your app preferences and cached data are stored locally on your device using secure storage methods provided by your operating system.

Offline Functionality

Madrid Parks works offline and stores essential information locally to ensure you always have access to park data, even without an internet connection.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect any information processed by the app, following industry best practices for mobile application security.

Your Rights and Choices

Location Permissions

You can control location access through your device settings:

  • Grant or revoke location permissions at any time
  • Choose between "While Using App" or "Always" permission
  • Disable location services completely

Data Deletion

Since we don't store personal data on our servers, simply uninstalling the app removes all locally stored information.

Opt-Out

You can opt out of anonymous usage statistics collection through the app's settings menu.

Children's Privacy

Madrid Parks is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your continued use of Madrid Parks after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

International Users

Madrid Parks is designed for use in Madrid, Spain. If you use the app from outside the European Union, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries with different data protection laws.
