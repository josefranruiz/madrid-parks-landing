Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Madrid Parks ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

🔒 Your Privacy Matters

Madrid Parks is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data and use it only to provide you with the best possible experience.

Information We Collect

Location Data

Madrid Parks requests access to your device's location to show nearby parks, calculate distances, and provide relevant weather and pollen information for your area.

Important: Your location data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

App Usage Data

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to improve the app's performance and user experience. This data does not identify you personally.

No Personal Information

Madrid Parks does not collect, store, or transmit personal identification information, user accounts, payment information, or social media data.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively to provide real-time park status information, display weather and environmental data, show pollen levels, improve app functionality, and ensure app stability.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties.

Data Sources

Madrid Parks integrates with public data sources from Madrid City Council and Regional Government. All data sources are publicly available and do not require authentication.

Data Storage and Security

Your app preferences and cached data are stored locally on your device using secure storage methods provided by your operating system.

Madrid Parks works offline and stores essential information locally to ensure you always have access to park data, even without an internet connection.

Your Rights and Choices

You can control location access through your device settings, grant or revoke permissions at any time, and disable location services completely.

Since we don't store personal data on our servers, simply uninstalling the app removes all locally stored information.

Children's Privacy

Madrid Parks is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
